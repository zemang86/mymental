# Understanding Anxiety
# Memahami Kebimbangan

## What is Anxiety? / Apakah Kebimbangan?

Anxiety is a normal response to stress, but when it becomes excessive and persistent, it can interfere with daily life. Anxiety disorders are the most common mental health conditions worldwide.

Kebimbangan adalah tindak balas normal terhadap tekanan, tetapi apabila ia menjadi berlebihan dan berterusan, ia boleh mengganggu kehidupan harian. Gangguan kebimbangan adalah keadaan kesihatan mental yang paling biasa di seluruh dunia.

## Types of Anxiety / Jenis-jenis Kebimbangan

### Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD) / Gangguan Kebimbangan Umum
Excessive worry about everyday things like health, money, work, or family.
Kebimbangan berlebihan tentang perkara harian seperti kesihatan, wang, kerja, atau keluarga.

### Social Anxiety / Kebimbangan Sosial
Fear of social situations and being judged by others.
Takut situasi sosial dan dinilai oleh orang lain.

### Panic Disorder / Gangguan Panik
Sudden episodes of intense fear with physical symptoms like racing heart and difficulty breathing.
Episod ketakutan yang sengit secara tiba-tiba dengan gejala fizikal seperti jantung berdebar dan sukar bernafas.

## Common Symptoms / Gejala Biasa

### Physical / Fizikal
- Racing heart / Jantung berdebar
- Sweating / Berpeluh
- Trembling / Menggeletar
- Shortness of breath / Sesak nafas
- Muscle tension / Ketegangan otot
- Stomach upset / Sakit perut

### Mental / Mental
- Excessive worry / Kebimbangan berlebihan
- Difficulty concentrating / Sukar menumpukan perhatian
- Irritability / Mudah marah
- Sleep problems / Masalah tidur
- Feeling on edge / Rasa gelisah

## When Anxiety Becomes a Problem / Bila Kebimbangan Menjadi Masalah

Anxiety becomes a disorder when:
- It is out of proportion to the situation / Ia tidak sepadan dengan situasi
- It interferes with daily activities / Ia mengganggu aktiviti harian
- It lasts for extended periods / Ia berlanjutan untuk tempoh yang lama
- It causes significant distress / Ia menyebabkan tekanan yang ketara

## Treatment Options / Pilihan Rawatan

Anxiety disorders are highly treatable. Common approaches include:
Gangguan kebimbangan sangat boleh dirawat. Pendekatan biasa termasuk:

- Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) / Terapi Tingkah Laku Kognitif
- Medication when recommended / Ubat apabila disyorkan
- Relaxation techniques / Teknik relaksasi
- Lifestyle changes / Perubahan gaya hidup
